Wait... It's not another button library. While searching for button components with smooth animations and dynamic interactions, I realized there weren't many options available. So, I decided to build my own. Built with <Link href="https://motion.dev" _blank={true}>Motion</Link> and <Link href="https://shadcn.com/ui" _blank={true}>Shadcn</Link>.

## The Problem

EvilButtons is built by <Link href="https://x.com/radiumcoders" _blank={true}>Jay</Link>. As a design engineer, I see ugly and common buttons everywhere, and no one in the community is creating a beautiful button library.

There are tons of button libraries out there, but none of them are beautiful - even though they allow customization, I see that no one is customizing them and they just use them as provided via docs.

So, here I'm building a beautiful buttons component website, open source, open code, it's all yours...

## Why I named it EvilButtons?

"Evil" doesn't mean malicious. It means:

- Unapologetically opinionated UI choices
- Design-first, animated, unique designs
- Handcrafted with SVGs and <Link href="https://motion.dev" _blank={true}>Motion</Link> to stand out

## Built on Motion

While finalising the design for EvilButtons, I wanted a good animation library. I went over tons of animation libraries and found that Motion has amazing features and is easy to use in every project.
